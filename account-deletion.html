Account Deletion for Re-Source

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

Re-Source respects your right to control your data. This page provides detailed information about what happens when you delete your account, including which data is deleted and which data may be retained.

1. How to Delete Your Account

Users can delete their account directly in the Re-Source app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Re-Source app
  2. Navigate to Profile
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your choice when prompted

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming your choice.

2. Data Deletion Policy

2.1 Data Immediately and Permanently Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is immediately and permanently deleted from our Firebase infrastructure:

  • User account and authentication credentials: Your login information and account identifiers from Firebase Authentication
  • Email address and profile information: All personal profile details (email, name, profile picture URL) associated with your account
  • All user-generated content: This includes:
    • All resources (titles, descriptions, URLs) stored in Cloud Firestore
    • All categories (names, colors, organizational preferences) stored in Cloud Firestore
    • All images uploaded to Firebase Storage
  • All personal data stored in our database: Any other personal information linked to your account in Cloud Firestore
  • User ID associations: Your unique user identifier and all data linked to it

Retention Period: 0 days (immediate deletion)

Your personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion confirmation. No personal user data is retained after account deletion.

2.2 Data That May Be Retained

While all personal data is deleted immediately, certain anonymized or system data may be retained for limited periods by third-party services:

Data Type Status Retention Period Description
Anonymized analytics data Retained Up to 14 months Fully anonymized usage statistics managed by Firebase Analytics that cannot be linked to you
Crash reports Retained Up to 90 days Anonymized diagnostic data managed by Firebase Crashlytics, used for improving app stability
Advertising data Retained 60-90 days Managed by Google AdMob according to Google's policies
System logs Retained Up to 30 days Maintained by Firebase for service integrity and security purposes

2.3 Important Notes About Retained Data

  • Cannot Identify You: All retained data is either fully anonymized or consists of system logs that cannot identify individual users
  • No Personal Information: Retained data does not contain any personal information such as email addresses, usernames, or user-generated content
  • Third-Party Services: Data managed by third-party services follows their respective policies:
    • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Firebase Privacy Policy
    • Google AdMob: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Privacy Information
  • Service Improvement: Anonymized data is used solely for improving app performance, stability, and user experience
  • Advertising ID Reset: You can reset your advertising ID at any time through your device settings to limit ad tracking independently of account deletion

3. Data Deletion Summary

Category Deletion Status Timeline
Personal user data Deleted Immediate (0 days)
Account credentials Deleted Immediate (0 days)
Profile information Deleted Immediate (0 days)
User-generated content Deleted Immediate (0 days)
Resources and categories Deleted Immediate (0 days)

4. After Account Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to access the Re-Source app with your previous credentials
  • All your saved links and resources will be permanently removed
  • You can create a new account at any time, but your previous data cannot be recovered
  • If you have any backups or exports of your data, they remain under your control
