Privacy Policy for Re-Source

Last Updated: December 16, 2024

Re-Source is a smart link management app that transforms every saved URL into a sleek, interactive card—automatically organized into categories for quick and easy access. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account using Google Sign-In, we collect:

  • Email Address: Your Google account email
  • Name: Your name (if provided via Google Sign-In)
  • Profile Picture URL: Your Google profile picture
  • User ID: A unique identifier for your account
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens for maintaining your session

Storage: Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore

1.2 User-Generated Content

When you use Re-Source, you create and store the following information:

  • Resources: Titles, descriptions, and URLs you save
  • Categories: Category names, colors, and organizational preferences
  • Custom Images: Images you upload for your resources
  • Metadata: Page titles, descriptions, images, and other publicly available information from the URLs you save
  • Timestamps: Resource access timestamps (when added and last accessed)

Storage: Cloud Firestore (structured data), Firebase Storage (images)

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Re-Source, the following information is automatically collected:

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, screen views, user engagement metrics
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen size, platform (Android/iOS)
  • Approximate Location: Country-level location (not precise GPS coordinates)
  • IP Address: For security and service provision
  • Crash Data: Crash logs, stack traces, and device state information for debugging
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics and diagnostics

Collection Method: Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Enable you to save, organize, and access your links
  • Automatic Organization: Parse and categorize your saved links for easier management
  • Display Rich Previews: Show you interactive cards with metadata from your saved URLs
  • Improve the App: Understand how Re-Source is used to enhance features and user experience
  • Maintain Security: Protect against unauthorized access and ensure data integrity

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Cloud Storage

Re-Source uses Google Firebase services to store your data securely:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores all user-generated content (resources, categories) and metadata
  • Firebase Storage: Stores images uploaded by users

Server Location: Google Cloud Platform data centers (region may vary)

Data Transmission: All data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS and encrypted at rest
  • Access Controls: Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • Authentication: Secure Google Sign-In authentication mechanism
  • Regular Updates: Regular security updates through Firebase SDK updates

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy. Here's how your information may be shared:

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google (Firebase Services): All app data is stored and processed on Firebase/Google Cloud infrastructure as described in Section 5
  • Google AdMob & Ad Partners: Advertising and device data is shared with Google's ad network and advertising partners for ad delivery and personalization

4.2 What We Don't Do

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your user-generated content (resources, categories) with third parties except Google as our infrastructure provider
  • Share your data for purposes other than those described in this policy

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Re-Source, our users, or others

5. Third-Party Services

Re-Source uses the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

5.1 Firebase Services (Google)

We use various Firebase services provided by Google for core app functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication: User authentication and account security
  • Cloud Firestore: Database storage for app functionality
  • Firebase Storage: Image storage for resources
  • Firebase Analytics: Understanding app usage, improving features, and identifying bugs
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Bug tracking and app stability improvement

Data Collected: As described in Section 1

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.2 Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Re-Source uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob collects and processes:

  • Advertising ID: AAID on Android, IDFA on iOS
  • Device Information: Model, OS version, screen size
  • IP Address: For approximate location
  • Ad Interaction Data: Views, clicks, and engagement
  • App Usage Data: To improve ad relevance

Purpose: Serving personalized advertisements and ad performance measurement

Data Sharing: AdMob shares data with Google's advertising partners and ad networks

User Control: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Disable Personalized Ads

Privacy Policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob Privacy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

5.3 Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In for simplified user authentication:

  • Data Collected: Email, profile name, profile picture, Google account ID
  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.4 External Links via WebView

When you open resource links through the app's WebView:

  • The destination website may collect standard web browsing data (IP address, user agent, cookies)
  • Important: Re-Source does not control or receive data from these external websites
  • Each website has its own privacy policy and data practices

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: View all the links and metadata you've saved in Re-Source
  • Modification: Edit or update any custom metadata or categories at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual links or all your data from the app
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data. For detailed information, see our Account Deletion Policy
  • Export: Export your saved links and data in a portable format (if available)

7. Data Retention

We retain different types of data for varying periods:

  • User Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • User-Generated Content: Retained until you delete it or delete your account
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Firebase Analytics defaults (typically up to 14 months)
  • Crash Logs: Retained for up to 90 days (Firebase Crashlytics default)
  • Advertising Data: Managed by Google AdMob, retained for 60-90 days per Google's policies
  • System Logs: Maintained by Firebase for up to 30 days for service integrity

For detailed information about account deletion and data retention, please see our Account Deletion Policy.

8. Data We Do Not Collect

To protect your privacy, Re-Source does not collect the following types of data:

  • Precise Geolocation: We do not collect GPS coordinates or precise location data
  • Contacts or Phone Numbers: We do not access your contact list or phone numbers
  • SMS or Call Logs: We do not access your text messages or call history
  • Microphone or Camera Data: Except when you explicitly choose to upload images
  • Calendar Data: We do not access your calendar
  • Photos: Except when you explicitly select images for upload
  • Health Data: We do not collect any health or fitness information
  • Financial Information: We do not collect payment or banking information
  • Social Connections: We do not access social connections outside of Google authentication

9. Children's Privacy

Re-Source is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of Re-Source after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a location different from your country of residence. By using Re-Source, you consent to such transfer and processing in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
