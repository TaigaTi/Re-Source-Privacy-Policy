Privacy Policy for Re-Source

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use Re-Source, you may provide us with the following information:

  • URLs and Links: The web addresses you save to the app
  • Custom Metadata: Any titles, descriptions, tags, or notes you manually add to your saved links
  • Categories: Any custom categories or organizational structures you create

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you save a URL, Re-Source automatically collects:

  • Metadata: Page titles, descriptions, images, and other publicly available information from the URLs you save
  • Timestamps: The date and time when you save or modify links
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as which features you use

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Enable you to save, organize, and access your links
  • Automatic Organization: Parse and categorize your saved links for easier management
  • Display Rich Previews: Show you interactive cards with metadata from your saved URLs
  • Improve the App: Understand how Re-Source is used to enhance features and user experience
  • Maintain Security: Protect against unauthorized access and ensure data integrity

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Re-Source primarily stores your data locally on your device. Your saved links, custom metadata, and categories remain on your device and are under your control.

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Re-Source, our users, or others

5. Third-Party Services

When you save a URL, Re-Source may access that website to retrieve metadata (title, description, images). This process:

  • Only accesses publicly available information from the URLs you choose to save
  • Does not share your identity or personal information with third-party websites
  • Is performed solely to enhance your experience within Re-Source

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: View all the links and metadata you've saved in Re-Source
  • Modification: Edit or update any custom metadata or categories at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual links or all your data from the app
  • Export: Export your saved links and data in a portable format (if available)

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you use Re-Source or as necessary to provide you with our services. You may delete your data at any time through the app's settings or by uninstalling the application.

8. Children's Privacy

Re-Source is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of Re-Source after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. International Users

Re-Source may be accessed by users from various countries around the world. Please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a location different from your country of residence. By using Re-Source, you consent to such transfer and processing in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
